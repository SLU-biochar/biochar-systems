Biochar systems analysis

An interactive flowchart

The flowchart above describes a biochar system, starting with the production of biomass and finishing with the end-of-life of the biochar product. Outside of the boundaries, a reference system is depicted.

Each box or process in this flowchart is a link. By clicking on it, you will access a dedicated page with definitions, explanations, useful resources and references. Disclaimer: the goal of this knowledge compilation is not to provide all information, but specifically the information needed to asess biochar systems for environmental purposes or decision-making based on environmental condiserations.

On each page, the information appears by increasing level of complexity: at the top, you will find definitions and overview information. The more you scroll down, specialised concepts are explained and other useful resources are provided. The levels of complexity are marked in each sections, from easy (*) to advanced (***).

Note: The generic flowchart results from our experience in performing LCA of various biochar systems. It is further described in our research article about assessing the co-benefits of biochar (Azzi et al. 2021).
